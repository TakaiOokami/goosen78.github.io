DMI Project Failures

Bringing the project in-house

-Wasted a huge amount of licence payers' money

-Estimated ¬£125.9m spent on DMI

Confusion and a lack of planning

-Executives failed to take control

-Far too complacent about project risks

-No single individual had overall responsibility

-Triple constraint was effectively ignored or not filled out

Lessons Learned

  • Importance of Senior management ownership

  • Failure to communicate well with Stakeholders & the Suppliers

  • Quit when the project failures became obvious

  • Failed the triple constraint

  • Project Schedule Management
